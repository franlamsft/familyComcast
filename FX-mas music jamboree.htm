FX-mas music jamboree

If you want to listen to what was originally in the score here are three .mp3 renditions. Note the addition of at least a tiny beat. My suggestion was for someone to keep time by hitting a box with stick and another pie plate with screws in it as the "cymbal". In these renditions you have real drum sounds.

Also these computer musicians use a lot of vibrato - possibly too much.
